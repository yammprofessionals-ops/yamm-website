About YAMM



Vision: To raise a generation of spiritually grounded, socially responsible, and mission-oriented Adventist youth.
Founded in 2020, YAMM is a structured youth evangelistic and mentorship ministry rooted in the Seventh-day Adventist Church. The ministry mobilizes students, young professionals and church youth for organized mission outreach, discipleship and community transformation across Kenya.
